Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enable location for prayer times?

Go to your iPhone Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services > Islamic Companion and select "While Using the App". Alternatively, you can set your location manually in the app's prayer times section.

Why is the Qibla compass not accurate?

Make sure you've calibrated your phone's compass by moving it in a figure-8 pattern. Also, stay away from metal objects or electronic devices that can interfere with the magnetometer.

How does the Sujud Counter work?

The app uses your phone's proximity sensor. When you place your phone near your forehead during sujud (prostration), it automatically detects and counts it. You can also switch to manual tap mode in the settings.
